# globalcvpro-privacy-policy
Privacy Policy for the GlobalCV Pro Android application.
Privacy Policy
GlobalCV Pro
Last updated: January 2025
GlobalCV Pro (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the GlobalCV Pro mobile application.
By using this application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
Information We Collect
Personal Information
When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may receive basic account information such as your name, email address, and profile picture. This information is used only for authentication and account identification.
User-Provided Data
The app allows users to create and manage CV/resume data. All information entered by the user is stored securely and is used only to provide app functionality.
Automatically Collected Information
We may collect certain information automatically, including:
Device type and operating system
App usage statistics
Advertising identifiers
How We Use Information
Collected information is used to:
Authenticate users
Save and sync CV data
Provide app features and functionality
Improve user experience
Display advertisements
Maintain app security and stability
Third-Party Services
GlobalCV Pro uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
These services include:
Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore)
Google AdMob (Advertisements)
Google Play Billing (In-app purchases)
Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.
Advertising
The app uses Google AdMob to display banner, rewarded, and interstitial ads.
AdMob may use device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
Users can manage ad preferences through their device settings.
Data Storage and Security
User data is stored securely using Firebase cloud services.
Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect user data; however, no system is completely secure.
User Control
Users can:
Sign out of the app at any time
Manage or edit their CV information
Control personalized ads through device settings
Children’s Privacy
GlobalCV Pro is not intended for children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
Permissions
The app may request permissions necessary for functionality, including:
Internet access
Billing access
Advertising services
Permissions are used strictly for their intended purpose.
Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately after being posted.
Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
Contact
Support and assistance are available within the application.
End of Policy
